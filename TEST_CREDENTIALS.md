## How to Generate Your Own Data

If you create a new account:

1. **Sign up** with your own credentials
2. **Complete consent** and **onboarding**
3. **Go to Daily Log** page
4. **Log your daily activities** (transport, electricity, food, etc.)
5. **Return to Dashboard** to see your charts populate!

The backend automatically calculates carbon emissions based on your daily log entries.
